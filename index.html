Who Is This For?

Sponsio

is for individuals who are in need of support and positive reinforcement when at risk of relapse. A few words of encouragement and understanding during times of isolation can mean the difference between making a good or bad decision.

Sponsio is for people who may know they have a problem but feel they have nobody they can talk to about it for fear of repercussion or punishment. Sponsio is a judgment free zone and offers the wisdom of volunteers who truly understand the burden of addiction.

Please note that we are not a replacement for counseling or long-term medical treatment. Our goal is to help you move from an emotionally overwhelming place to a sense of calm, at which point you feel ready to continue on your own.

Community Support

Challenges Happen

Recovery is a whole lot more than getting clean. Some of the biggest hurdles occur when tough life events cause us to question sobriety. That's when you need to talk to someone.

People Who Care

We believe nobody should ever feel that they have no one to talk to. If you aren't comfortable sharing with friends or family you can trust that there is someone who cares just a text away.

Finding Purpose

Our hope is that the people who need this service today will be the volunteers on the line tomorrow. Helping one another succeed gives us purpose and reminds us what life is about.
